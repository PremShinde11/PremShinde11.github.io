// UNIVERSITY MODULE · TRUSTWORTHY AI
TruXplain — Conceptual Architecture for Trustworthy Recommender Systems

Recommender systems are foundational to user experience across e-commerce, education, healthcare, and digital media. This report presents TruXplain, exploring six foundational trust dimensions: explainability, fairness, robustness, privacy, transparency, and user agency. TruXplain blends hybrid collaborative and content-based filtering, emotion-driven analysis, federated learning for privacy, behavioural pattern mining, fairness re-ranking, meta-learning for cold-start, and interactive counterfactual + visual explanations. A trust telemetry layer continuously monitors fairness and robustness for ongoing accountability — moving beyond accuracy toward systems users can genuinely trust.
